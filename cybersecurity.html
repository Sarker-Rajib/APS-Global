Cybersecurity

    APS Global’s CMMC and Research offices are on the grounds of Capitol Technology University, an NSA & DHS Center of Excellence. APS Global’s founder, Dr. Richard Hansen CISSP/CMMC-PA, designs and teaches cybersecurity courses, is a Dissertation Chair for Doctoral students, and performs research.


    Dr. Hansen coaches the university’s Cybersecurity Team and speaks at DEF CON and other cybersecurity conferences. His research includes leading a student team in identifying LIDAR vulnerabilities for autonomous vehicles , and developing a simple and low-cost method of detecting the encryption vulnerability involved in the Zoom Hack of 2020.
